1.Design

1.1Exterior Styling:

*Sporty and modern design with bold lines.

*LED projector headlamps with LED daytime running lights (DRLs).

*Distinctive front grille and sleek alloy wheels.

*Available in vibrant color options.

1.2Interior Design:

*Ergonomically designed dashboard and control layout.

*Sporty instrument cluster with MID (Multi-Information Display).

*Premium fabric upholstery and comfortable seating.

*Ample legroom and headroom for passengers.

2.Performance

2.1Engine:

*Efficient petrol engines, often with Dualjet technology for better performance and fuel economy.

*Engine options may include 1.2-liter K12M or 1.2-liter K12N Dualjet.

2.2Transmission:

*5-speed manual transmission.

*Optional AGS (Auto Gear Shift) for automatic convenience.

3.Safety:

3.1Structural Integrity:

*HEARTECT platform for enhanced structural rigidity and safety.

*Dual front airbags and ABS with EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution) as standard.

3.2Advance Safety Features:

*Reverse parking sensors and camera.

*ISOFIX child seat anchors.

*Hill Hold Assist (in automatic variants).
